Between 1916 and 1945, the Hotel Bristol was so large that it extended the entire block from Kärntner Ring 1 to 7. At that time the hotel still boasted the Grill Room, a Titanic-style dining room. Neither the room nor the ship exists any longer. With its prominent address of Kärntner Ring 1, today the Hotel Bristol is the hotel next to the Vienna State Opera, with 150 rooms and suites. The rooms and suites at Hotel Bristol, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Vienna are designed in a luxurious Art Deco style by renowned designer Pierre Yves Rochon.Our 5 star hotel's two imposing round towers are home to rooms and suites unlike any found elsewhere in the world. Take in far-reaching
views of Vienna, Austria from the roof terraces and sleep beneath the hand-painted starry ceiling of the tower suite.The famous Prince of Wales
Suite offers our guests pure hotel luxury with a living room, library, study and private sauna. The Bristol Lounge, opposite the Vienna State
Opera, forms the culinary component of the legendary Hotel Bristol Vienna. Open all day, like the historic hotel, it is presented in an
elegant Art Deco style. Local delicacies, exquisite specialties and international classics are served in a cozy salon atmosphere and
historical ambience. Accessible from Mahlerstraße or directly from the Hotel Bristol, the Bristol Lounge sets new standards for
modern hospitality.
