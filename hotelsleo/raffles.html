Cuisines:

Every morning, the Parisian Palace offers a delicious buffet with carefully selected products. Tasty creations are prepared by Quentin Lechat and guests can enjoy organic eggs, muesli, selections from by the french baker Frédéric Lalos, organic tea, freshly-squeezed fruit juice, gluten-free selections… The brunches are gourmet adventures, elevated with select products from talented and passionate craftsmen, including a vast selection of desserts by Pastry Chef Quentin Lechat. Every Saturday during brunch, the most artistic Parisian Palace offers creative and playful activities for its youngest guests. Born on the Japanese island of Hokkaido, Hideki Endo grew up learning everything there is to know about hospitality and service. His family ran a hotel and his father was a chef, so it was no surprise when young Hideki fell in love with the art of cooking. For nine years he trained as a sushi master in the city’s finest establishments. In 2006 he joined Nobu for the opening of the Hong Kong InterContinental. Now Executive Chef at Matsuhisa Paris, Hideki Endo is playing his part in bringing Nobu Matsuhisa’s take on Japanese cuisine to a French audience.
