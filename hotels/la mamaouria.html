Recognition:

La Mamounia was voted “Best Hotel” in the Middle East and Africa in 2018, by the prestigious magazine Condé Nast Traveller.

2020 LA Mamounia revamped : Entirely redesigned, the Palace restaurants welcomed two distinguished Chefs – Jean-Georges Vongerichten and Pierre Hermé – whose creations are now served on every menu at La Mamounia.

As a veritable sanctuary dedicated to beauty and wellness, the Spa at La Mamounia was awarded the title of “Best Spa” for 5 successive years (2010 to 2015) by the prestigious American magazine Spa Finder.

In memory of Winston Churchill, La Mamounia organised an exhibition of his paintings, revealing Churchill’s works for the first time ever in Morocco (most of them had never left England) alongside paintings from the Moroccan artist Hassan El Glaoui.
