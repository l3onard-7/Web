Membership programmes

  • The Membership Programme is for travel companies that make hotel bookings on behalf of individuals and companies. As a member your organisation will earn points for eligible materialised reservations at participating hotels. These accumulated points can later be redeemed for holidays, dining vouchers or gift certificates. A member organisation will earn 1 point for every INR 9,000 of revenue materialised, excluding taxes, at a participating property. For hotel stays of over 30 days, points will be earned only for the first 30 days of stay.
