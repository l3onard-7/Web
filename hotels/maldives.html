Discover a secluded getaway at our Marriott resort

A hidden gem in the Indian Ocean, the Marriott Resort Maldives allows you to escape from the world and relax in style. Our expert staff will arrange for your transport so that you can easily reach the hotel’s island. During your visit, enjoy world-class snorkeling and diving along with some of the best places on earth to see whale sharks. You can visit local islands like Dhigurah or Maamigili or enjoy diving on site at our Dive and Water Sports Center. Relax with a glass of champagne in your villa’s private pool as you share your favorite memories from seeing the coral reefs at Fenfushi.
